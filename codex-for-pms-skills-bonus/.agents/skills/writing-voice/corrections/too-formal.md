# Correction: Too Formal

**What was wrong:** First draft used phrases like "I would recommend" and "It is advisable to consider."

**How to fix:** Use direct language. "Do this" not "I would recommend doing this." "Try X" not "It is advisable to consider X."

**Example:**
- Before: "I would recommend implementing a phased rollout to mitigate potential risks."
- After: "Roll it out in phases. Less risk that way."
