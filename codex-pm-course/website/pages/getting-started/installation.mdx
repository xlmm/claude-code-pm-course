# Module 0.2: Get the Codex App
## Reference Guide

- **Time to Complete:** 5 minutes
- **Prerequisites:** Mac or Windows + a ChatGPT account

## 📖 Overview

You'll download the **Codex app** and sign in with your ChatGPT account. That's it — it installs like any other app, and once you're signed in you're ready to open the course.

**Key takeaway:** Download the app from [openai.com/codex](https://openai.com/codex/), open it, choose **Sign in with ChatGPT**.

## Step 1: Download the App

**[⬇️ Get Codex at openai.com/codex →](https://openai.com/codex/)**

Click the download button for your platform — Codex is available for **Mac** and **Windows**.

{/* screenshot: codex-app-download.png — the openai.com/codex download page */}

## Step 2: Install and Open It

It installs like any other app:

- **Mac:** open the downloaded file and drag **Codex** into your Applications folder, then launch it.
- **Windows:** run the installer and follow the prompts, then launch Codex.

## Step 3: Sign In

1. When Codex opens, choose **"Sign in with ChatGPT"** (recommended) and complete the login in your browser.
2. That's it — Codex now uses your ChatGPT subscription.

{/* screenshot: codex-app-signin.png — the app sign-in screen */}

> Prefer pay-as-you-go? You can sign in with an OpenAI **API key** instead (billed per use). For this course, signing in with ChatGPT is simpler.

## Step 4: Pick a Plan

Codex is included across ChatGPT plans. You can start free; most PMs are comfortable on **Plus**.

| Plan | Price | Good for |
|------|-------|----------|
| **Free** | $0 | Trying Codex on light tasks |
| **Go** | $8/mo | Occasional, lightweight use |
| **Plus** | $20/mo | **Recommended** — daily PM work |
| **Pro** | $100+/mo | Heavy use, much higher limits |

Usage limits and model availability change often — check the **[current pricing page](https://developers.openai.com/codex/pricing)** for the latest. If you hit a limit, Codex lets you top up with credits without upgrading.

## A Note on Permissions

The first time Codex makes changes, it asks for permission. By default it works in **Auto** mode — it reads, edits, and runs tasks **inside your current project folder**, and asks before touching anything outside it. That's exactly right for this course.

Codex always shows you what it did, so nothing happens silently. You can review every change.

## 🐛 Troubleshooting

**Sign-in browser window doesn't open**
- Quit and reopen the Codex app, then try signing in again.

**Not sure which plan you're on**
- Check [chatgpt.com](https://chatgpt.com) → Settings → Subscription. Any plan works for starting the course.

**The app won't open (Mac)**
- If macOS blocks it, go to System Settings → Privacy & Security and click **Open Anyway**.
