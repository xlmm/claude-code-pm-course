# Interview 01 - Marcus

Marcus is an engineering manager at a 120-person remote company.

## Notes

- Marcus said setup felt "mostly blank" after signup.
- His team wanted templates for common workflows.
- He invited two engineers, but neither knew what to do next.
- He expected onboarding to recommend a Workspace structure based on team type.

## Quote

"I do not need another empty tool. I need something that helps my team get moving in the first ten minutes."
