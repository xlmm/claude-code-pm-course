Coffee Shop Failure Rate Analysis
Restaurant Business Online Industry Study

Survival Statistics:
- Year 1 survival rate: 72%
- Year 3 survival rate: 48%
- Year 5 survival rate: 34%
- Year 10 survival rate: 21%

Note: Coffee shop survival exceeds general restaurant industry (60% fail year 1)

Primary Failure Causes (ranked by frequency):

1. Undercapitalization (34% of failures)
   - Average shortfall: $45,000 in operating capital
   - Critical period: months 4-8 when initial capital depleted

2. Poor location selection (28%)
   - Insufficient foot traffic: 67% of location failures
   - Parking issues: 22%
   - Competition density: 11%

3. Operational inefficiency (18%)
   - Labor cost overruns: 54% of operational failures
   - Inventory waste: 31%
   - Equipment issues: 15%

4. Market misread (12%)
   - Concept mismatch with area demographics
   - Pricing misalignment
   - Competition underestimation

5. Owner burnout (8%)
   - Average work week before failure: 72 hours
   - Time to burnout indicators: 14 months

Success Correlates:
- Prior food service experience: +40% survival
- Formal business plan: +28% survival
- 12+ months operating capital: +52% survival
- Location with 10k+ daily foot traffic: +35% survival
