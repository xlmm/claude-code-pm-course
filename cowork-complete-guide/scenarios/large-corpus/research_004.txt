Mobile Ordering Adoption in Coffee Retail
Square Payment Analytics Report 2025

Data Source: 8,900 coffee shops using Square POS

Overall Mobile Order Penetration:
- 2023: 18% of transactions
- 2024: 27% of transactions
- 2025: 36% of transactions
- Projected 2026: 44%

Order Channel Distribution (2025):
- In-store POS: 54%
- Mobile app orders: 24%
- Third-party delivery apps: 12%
- Web ordering: 6%
- Kiosk: 4%

Mobile User Demographics:
- Under 25: 52% use mobile ordering
- 25-40: 44% use mobile ordering
- 40-55: 28% use mobile ordering
- Over 55: 11% use mobile ordering

Operational Impacts:
- Average transaction time reduction: 62%
- Peak hour throughput increase: 28%
- Order accuracy improvement: 34%
- Average ticket size: +12% vs in-store

Implementation Costs:
- Basic integration: $200-500/month
- Full-featured platform: $500-1,500/month
- Custom app development: $25,000-75,000

Abandonment Rates:
- Cart abandonment: 23% (industry average for food)
- Primary reasons: minimum order requirements (34%), delivery fees (29%), long wait times (21%)
