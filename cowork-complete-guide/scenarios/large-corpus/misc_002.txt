Equipment Vendor Comparison Notes
Research compiled: October 2025

Espresso Machines:

La Marzocco (Italy)
- Models: Linea PB, GB5, Strada
- Reputation: Industry gold standard
- Service: Strong authorized network
- Lead time: 4-8 weeks
- Resale value: Excellent (retains 60-70%)

Victoria Arduino (Italy, Simonelli Group)
- Models: Eagle One, Black Eagle
- Reputation: Rising premium brand
- Service: Through Nuova Simonelli network
- Lead time: 2-4 weeks
- Resale value: Good but newer market presence

Synesso (USA, Seattle)
- Models: MVP, S-series
- Reputation: Craft-focused, customizable
- Service: Limited outside major metros
- Lead time: 8-12 weeks (made to order)
- Resale value: Strong in specialty market

Grinders:

Mahlkonig (Germany)
- Models: EK43, E65S, E80
- The standard for specialty shops
- $2,500-4,500 range

Mazzer (Italy)
- Models: Robur, Major, Kony
- Reliable workhorses
- $1,500-3,000 range

Eureka (Italy)
- Models: Atom, Mignon
- Growing in specialty segment
- $600-1,800 range

Recommendation priority: Reliability > Features > Price
Service network accessibility is critical factor
Budget 15% of machine cost annually for maintenance
