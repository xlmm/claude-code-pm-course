Local vs Chain Coffee Shop Preference Study
Morning Consult Consumer Research - December 2025

Survey: 5,200 US adults, nationally representative sample

Overall Preference (Primary coffee shop choice):
- Local/independent: 38%
- Regional chain: 24%
- National chain: 31%
- No preference: 7%

Preference by Factor (% citing as "very important"):

Local Shop Advantages:
- Supporting local business: 67%
- Coffee quality: 58%
- Unique atmosphere: 54%
- Community connection: 51%
- Ethical sourcing transparency: 47%
- Staff knowledge: 44%

Chain Advantages:
- Consistency: 63%
- Convenience/locations: 58%
- Speed of service: 52%
- Mobile ordering: 48%
- Loyalty rewards: 44%
- Price: 38%

Switching Behavior:
- 34% switched primary shop in past year
- Top reasons for switching to local: quality (42%), values alignment (31%)
- Top reasons for switching to chain: convenience (47%), rewards (28%)

Willingness to Travel:
- For preferred local shop: 12 minutes average
- For any coffee shop: 6 minutes average
- "Worth a trip" threshold for quality: 15 minutes

Price Sensitivity:
- Would pay $1+ more for preferred shop: 54%
- Would pay $2+ more: 31%
- Would pay $3+ more: 12%
