# TaskFlow Live: Quick 2-Week Launch Timeline

## Week 1: Target Outreach & Beta
- **Day 1-2**: Finalize Outreach List. Sarah (Sales) identifies top Enterprise accounts.
- **Day 3**: Launch VIP Announcement Emails. Send tailored emails to decision-makers.
- **Day 4-5**: High-Touch Onboarding. Support team assists target accounts with activation.

## Week 2: Activation & Calibration
- **Day 6-7**: Collect Real-Time Feedback. Daily syncs with Sarah to monitor account engagement.
- **Day 8-9**: Refine Messaging. Update in-app copy based on enterprise feedback.
- **Day 10**: GA Readiness Recap. Review metrics (conflict reduction, DAU) for full rollout.

## Success Checkpoints
- **End of Week 1**: 20% activation rate among target Enterprise accounts.
- **End of Week 2**: <5 minute average response time for launch-related support tickets.
