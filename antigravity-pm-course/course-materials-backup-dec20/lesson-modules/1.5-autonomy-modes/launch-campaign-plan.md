# TaskFlow Live: Launch Campaign Plan

## Campaign Objective
Maximize adoption among enterprise teams and showcase TaskFlow's evolution into a real-time collaboration platform.

## Target Audience
- **Primary**: Enterprise teams (PM leads, engineering managers)
- **Key Persona**: Sarah Chen (TechCorp PM)

## Messaging Pillars
1. **PM-Specific**: Built for product workflows (PRDs, roadmapping), not just engineering.
2. **Enterprise-Grade**: Performance at 50+ concurrent users with robust security.
3. **Smart Collaboration**: Contextual updates without the notification "firehose."

## Lead Channel: Enterprise VIP Email
### Subject Lines (A/B Test)
- **Option A**: [VIP Early Access] TaskFlow is now multiplayer
- **Option B**: See your team's work, live: Introducing TaskFlow Live

### Email Core Message
"TaskFlow Live brings your distributed team together with real-time editing, live cursors, and presence indicators. Built for the complexity of enterprise product management."

## Stakeholder Strategy
- **Sales (Sarah)**: Identify top 10 at-risk or high-potential enterprise accounts for VIP outreach.
- **Support**: Priority channel for high-touch feedback from enterprise beta users.
